Find Your Study Group Today With Group Mii.

With our service, we offer you an easy way to find similar people to learn new things together. Create or Join a study group of 3 to 6 people in it and start your new journey with your new group to achieve greatness and create new things.

About Us

We as a company offer this service to our users to enjoy learning together in a safe environment where they could motivate each other to achieve new things. The company is founded by Abdalla Khaled El-Mogy in 2020 with one purpose in mind, Offering people a new chance of learning new things with higher motivation as proven in Psychology that a person works more and achieve more when others rely on them.
